# Account Management — Kairos

## Account Center

Access your account at `/app/account`.

### Tabs

- **Profile** — Account details and sign out
- **Security** — Password and connected accounts
- **Billing** — Subscription and payment
- **Usage** — Current usage and limits
- **API Keys** — Generate and manage API keys
- **Notifications** — System notifications

## Profile

- Email address (from authentication)
- Connected accounts (GitHub, Google)
- Sign out

## Security

- Change password (Settings → Security)
- Connected accounts (GitHub OAuth)
- Session management

## Billing

- Current plan and renewal date
- Upgrade/downgrade
- Payment methods (via Stripe billing portal)
- Invoice history (via Stripe billing portal)

## Usage

Real-time usage tracking:
- Knowledge bases
- Documents
- AI chats
- Uploads
- Storage

Progress bars show usage against plan limits.

## Notifications

- Subscription changes
- Payment failures
- System announcements
- Mark as read/unread

## Delete Account

Contact support to delete your account. This action is irreversible.

## Export Data

Contact support to export your account data.
